    OUR MISSION

    To promote early childhood development in the least restrictive environment.

    What We Do

    Our Objectives

    • To integrate children with disabilities with their able bodied peers
    • To offer early childhood education
    • To boost children’s potential through therapeutic intervention
    • To engage in community awareness and advocacy programmes
    • To offer parental and staff training in handling disabled children
    • To institute referrals for special education
